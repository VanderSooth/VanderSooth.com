VanderSooth Entertainment

VanderSooth Entertainment was born out of a deep belief in Daniel M. Pitt's compelling vision, powerfully realized in this first book. Our mission is to share this extraordinary story with the world, and we believe publication is the essential first step.

However, we are a team of passionate 'newbies' and amateurs in the publishing world. To attract a professional publisher, we have decided to produce a Kickstarter Campaign, backing a "Publisher's Manuscript" version of Book 1. We believe this book series has significant potential in the Youth Fantasy market, appealing to readers who enjoy fantasies in the fashion of "The Hobbit," or even more similarly, "The Harry Potter" Series, which matured with the reader base. A successful Kickstarter campaign will demonstrate this audience demand and provide a strong foundation for a fruitful publishing partnership.

The rich world and characters in this series lend themselves perfectly to adaptation into other entertainment mediums. We envision a potential theater production and a movie franchise, which is why we've named our company VanderSooth Entertainment. The inclusion of original songs in the book is a deliberate step towards this broader vision.

To demonstrate our commitment to education and community engagement, we are launching a Scholarship Contest. Initially, we're offering a prize of $500.00, awarded to winning applicants pursuing higher education in the United States. Our hope is that a successful Kickstarter will allow us to increase the prize amount and establish a non-profit dedicated to supporting future scholars.

We are a team of passionate 'newbies' and amateurs, doing our best with limited resources. We appreciate your patience with our initial, 'unrefined' products, including this website. With your support, we are confident that we can bring this extraordinary story to a wider audience and enhance its presentation in the future.
